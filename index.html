A Quick Glance at Us...

Our project is an e-learning platform based education providing service developed by some passionate developers from TIET, namely - Ayush, Akshat, Jashan & Sumit.
Our project is a solution providing easily accessible and affordable content to all students. Our aim through this website is to help make the process of learning entertaining and attractive to the student, especially in today's age.

This Website was developed using HTML(HyperText Markup Language), CSS(Cascading Style Sheet) and JavaScript(to support all our front-end requirements).
